How to use Jam Session

Use your mouse or keyboard to press the "keys" below to make sounds. Change the volume and kind of sounds using the interface to the right (click "save" for changes to go through). Play with other people by going to a "sound room" where you can jam with up to 4 other users. If you want to play by yourself, stay on the homepage.
